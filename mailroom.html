Automated Mailroom Management

Mailrooms play a very critical and important role in any companies day to day operations. Keeping the importance of the mailroom in mind Ksvl has evolved – Automated Mailroom Management, which provides a very defined and transparent work process while optimizing the costs for the corporates.

The software allows multi-functions and virtually paperless mailroom operations. Institutions from banks to hotels to universities are enjoying the automated mailroom benefits.

KSVL Mailroom Management Services Include :

  • Centralized Mail Management
  • Pre-sorted Mail Distribution and Office Services
  • Digital Mailroom Solutions
  • Package Tracking
  • Inter-office and Courier Distribution Services
  • Mail Scanning
  • Mail Presorting
  • Real-time data capture
  • Customizable and User Friendly
  • Increased efficiency in the mailroom
  • Reduced cost of operations
